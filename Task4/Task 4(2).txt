# Social Media Responsibility Checklist (For College Students)

##  Do’s
- Use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
- Give proper credit when sharing someone else’s work or ideas.
- Keep your profile clean and suitable for academic or professional viewing.
- Report inappropriate or harmful content when you see it.
- Limit your screen time and use social media productively.

##  Don’ts
- Don’t post when you are angry or emotional — it can lead to regret later.
- Don’t accept friend requests from unknown or suspicious accounts.
- Don’t click on unknown links that may lead to scams or phishing.
- Don’t use social media during classes or important academic work.
- Don’t compare your life with others — social media often shows only highlights.
